Getting Started

Surtax Oversight Pro provides two main views for managing government contracts and surtax-funded capital projects:

  • Surtax Oversight — Focus on surtax-funded capital projects, schools, and committee concerns
  • Contract Management — Full contract lifecycle, vendor performance, and executive analytics

Toggle between views using the switch at the top of the sidebar.

Personas

Committee Member

High-level oversight view with simplified navigation. See portfolio health, open concerns, and meeting preparation tools.

District Staff

Full access to analytics, detailed contract data, vendor management, risk monitoring, and compliance tools.

Switch personas using the dropdown in the top-right header bar.
